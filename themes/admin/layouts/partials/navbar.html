Cloud Run

weather-api
Service
Authenticated REST API. All mutations (cities CRUD, backfill trigger, sync, reset) go through here.
weather-polymarket
Job
Fetches Polymarket price history and writes snapshots to BigQuery. Runs daily at 01:00 UTC; also triggered by backfill and reset.
weather-sync
Job
Exports tracked_cities and polymarket_snapshots from BigQuery to GCS and GitHub. Runs daily at 03:00 UTC.
weather-meteo-ecmwf
Job
Fetches ECMWF IFS ensemble forecasts from Open-Meteo and writes to BigQuery (meteo_ecmwf_forecasts). Runs at 04:30 and 16:30 UTC.
weather-meteo-icon
Job
Fetches DWD ICON seamless ensemble forecasts from Open-Meteo and writes to BigQuery (meteo_icon_forecasts). Runs at 00:05, 06:05, 12:05, 18:05 UTC.
